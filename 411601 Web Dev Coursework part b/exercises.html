Creating a exercise routine



To maintain a good physical health, regular exercise is essential as it helps improve mobility, deal with stress and heal from common injuries.

However, it is important to create a balanced routine that suits your own needs and capabilities, furthermore something that you really enjoy and does not feel like a chore.
